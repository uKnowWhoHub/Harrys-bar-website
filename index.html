Our Story

A Wigan Institution Since 19??

Founded with a passion for great drinks and good company, Harry's Bar has been at the heart of Wigan's social scene for decades. What started as a modest pub has grown into a beloved establishment known for its warm atmosphere and exceptional service.

Our walls have witnessed countless celebrations, from birthday toasts to championship victories. The familiar clink of glasses and hum of conversation create the soundtrack to many cherished memories.

Today, under new ownership but with the same spirit, we continue the tradition of providing a welcoming space where everyone feels at home.
